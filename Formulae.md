# Mathematical Foundations — Transportation Systems (India)
### *(Extracted from “The Transportation Problem”)*

---

## 1. Core System Equation

```Sustainable Transport = Integration × Efficiency × Clean Energy × Intelligence ```

## 2. Fundamental Transport Function

```Mobility=f(EnergyInput,Efficiency,Technology)```

## 3. 
```Emissions=Activity×EmissionFactor```

Where:
- Activity = distance, trips, fuel consumption
- Emission Factor = emissions per unit activity

## 4. Extended Emission Equation
```FreightEmissions=Volume×Distance×Mode×EnergyIntensity×CarbonIntensity```

Where:
- Volume = tonnage
- Distance = km
- Mode = road, rail, air, sea
- Energy Intensity = energy per tonne km
- Carbon Intensity = CO2 per energy unit

## 5. Energy–Emission Relationship
```Co2=EnergyConsumption×EmissionFactor```

## 6. Lifecycle Emissions (LCA)
```TotalEmissions=Production+Operation+Disposal```

## 7. Transport Efficiency Model
```Efficiency=UsefulOutput/EnergyInput```

Where:
- Useful Output = distance, trips, tonnage
- Energy Input = energy consumption

## 8. System Efficiency Equation
```SystemEfficiency=TransportEfficiency×Integration×CleanEnergy```
```E=Output/(Energy+Time+Cost)```

Where:
- Transport Efficiency = efficiency of transport system
- Integration = integration of transport system
- Clean Energy = clean energy used in transport system
- Output = useful output of transport system
- Energy = energy consumption of transport system
- Time = time taken to complete task
- Cost = cost of transport system

## 9. Logistics Cost Function
```TotalCost=TransportCost+InventoryCost+HandlingCost```

Where:
- Transport Cost = cost of transport system
- Inventory Cost = cost of inventory system
- Handling Cost = cost of handling system

## 10. Energy Intensity
```EnergyIntensity=Energy/Passenger−km(ortonne−km)```

## 11. Accessibility Function (Gravity Model)
```A=Σ(Oj×e(−βcij))```

Where:
- A = accessibility
- Oj = opportunities at destination j
- e = exponential
- β = beta
- cij = travel cost

## 12. Travel Demand Model (Conceptual)
```Demand=TripGeneration+Distribution+ModeChoice+Assignment```

Where:
- Demand = travel demand
- Trip Generation = trip generation
- Distribution = distribution
- Mode Choice = mode choice
- Assignment = assignment

## 13. Land Use–Transport Interaction (LUTI)
```LandUse↔TransportDemand```

## 14. System Optimization Model
```Efficiency=(Coverage×Availability)/(Cost+Time+Friction)```

## 15. Sustainability Index Model
```SustainabilityIndex=w1E+w2C+w3S+w4P```

Where:
- E = Environmental
- C = Economic
- S = Social
- P = Performance

## 16. Demand–Supply Gap Relationship
```Demand>Supply→PrivateVehicleGrowth→Congestion→Emissions```

## 17. Transport Feedback Loop
```UrbanSprawl→LongerTrips→MoreVehicles→Congestion→Emissions→InfrastructureExpansion→MoreSprawl```

## 18. Electrification Impact Equation
```EVEmissions=ElectricityConsumption×GridEmissionFactor```

Where:
- EVEmissions = electric vehicle emissions
- ElectricityConsumption = electricity consumption
- GridEmissionFactor = grid emission factor

## 19. Freight Optimization Model
```Decarbonization=Efficiency+ModalShift+FuelTransition```

## 20. Decarbonization Equation
```Decarbonization=Electrification+CleanEnergy+Efficiency+ModalShift```

## 21. Modal Shift Impact
```Road→Rail⇒Energy↓⇒Emissions↓```

## 22. Digital Mobility Model
```Data→Processing→Insight→Action```

## 23. AI Mobility Equation
```FutureMobility=Data×Intelligence×Integration```

## 24. Integrated Transport System
```IntegratedMobility=Modes+Infrastructure+Data+Governance+Energy```

## 25. Urban Emissions Model
```UrbanEmissions=Transport+Energy+LandUse+Behavior```

Where:
- Transport = transport emissions
- Energy = energy emissions
- LandUse = land use emissions
- Behavior = behavior emissions

## 26. Policy Impact Equation
```PolicyImpact=Policy×Integration×Impact```
```Impact=Before−After```

Where:
- Policy = policy

- Impact = impact
